This webpage is to help people with trigonometry.

Objectives

  1. Information from teachers
  2. Look interesting, colorful
  3. Font is easy to read, not too big or small
  4. Include a navigation bar on the side, a search bar
  5. New tabs will open when clicking
  6. Viewable online PDF reader
  7. FOR students that are struggling with chapter 14, trigonometry TO understand and study by themselves by quizzes and quick tips.
  8. FOR Mr. Kang (real world client) TO teach his student and help them instead of creating a moodle page separately.
  9. FOR people outside of the classroom TO learn and understand also to have access about the trigonometry chapter information.

Evaluation

We came up with many different ideas, we finalized our decision. We are making a website for Mr. Kang to help him teach trig easier and people can get access to the information easier and learn faster.
